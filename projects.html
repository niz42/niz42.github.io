Invisible Kingdom
2023
Invisible Kingdom is an interactive light installation designed to highlight the impact of urbanization on the underground fungal network. Through their choices, the audience trigger different lighting effects that visualize the consequences of human activities on mycorrhizal networks, encouraging reflection on the relationship between urbanization and the natural world.
